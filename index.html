Read More

Read More is a chrome extension for quick word dictionary . Just by double click , Read More can give you definition , pronunciation & example of the selected word.
How does this work :
A background script will always be running under the hood . That will wait for different events and will connect current DOM(Document Object Model) with content script & popup script . On double click content script will get the selected text then it will call a fetch api for information about the text . The result will then be poped up on the screen .

Quick Reader

Quick Reader is html parser based application that can turn any obscure gibberish looking site to well ordered & Readable with tons of extra features.
How does this work :
With the help of readability library the main part of the body (Article) will be differentiated . Then Quick Reader will be used as a template ( frame) for inhancing reading experience. Moreover , Quick Reader will offer a tons of feature such as font , background , theme changing & search in text ,etc.
